Hello, I'm Die Chance, the Catlock guy!

Back in February, after being inspired by other artists who had done portrait mods for Deadlock, I played around with the idea of creating my own. After a couple of minutes of doodling, I posted my first batch of portraits, and the support and encouragement from the community was unlike anything I had received before in my many years as a digital fan artist.

After the completion of my most well known project, I've got to meet some really amazing creators and community members, and I've been deeply flattered by the overwhelming amount of attention my work has received. It's with great honor that Poshypop, his team and I take this project to the next level and continue providing for the community members who got me here!

If you're reading this, I can't express how overjoyed your support makes me, and I hope you're as excited as I am for the future. <3
