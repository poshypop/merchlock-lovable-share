About · Merchlock

Human artists should feel valued.

Merchlock gives Deadlock community artists a platform for real, ready-to-ship merch in a world where AI can make creative work feel disposable. Artists bring the designs; we handle production, the shop, and royalty routing.

01 · The Story

Why we started.

Merchlock supports the artists making Deadlock's community look the way it does: original designs, fan characters, hand-drawn style, and work made by real people. Our first collaborator is DIECHANCE, the catlock guy.

It is easy right now for artists to feel pushed aside by AI image generation, repost culture, and platforms that treat art like infinite content. We want Merchlock to be one reason artists keep making things for the communities they love.

Artists have historically been undervalued and underpaid. Our promise is simple: credit the artist, protect their creative control, turn their work into real products, and route real revenue back to them. For the CatLock collection, 30% of net sales revenue goes to the artist.

05 · Questions

The things people ask.

Is this official?

No. Merchlock is unofficial fan-made merch. We're not affiliated with the game's developer or publisher.

How do artists get paid?

For the CatLock collection, the artist receives 30% of net sales revenue. Net sales revenue excludes sales tax, shipping charges, payment processing fees, refunds/returns, and customs/import duties.

Do you make the plushies yourselves?

No. Artists create the designs; we partner with a small manufacturing factory to produce them. Samples go to the artist for approval.

Can I pitch a design?

Yes. We run an open call between voting rounds. Artists keep creative control, clear credit, and collection-specific royalty terms.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes. Free shipping on orders over $75. Returns accepted within 30 days.

How does voting work?

Five candidates per round. One vote per visitor. 14 days of voting. Highest vote count wins.
